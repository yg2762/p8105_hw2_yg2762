The `pols_month_df` contains 4932 observations of 4 variables related to
the number of national politicians who are democratic or republican at
any given time; the `snp_df` contains 787 observations of 3 variables of
the closing values of the S&P stock index on the associated month; the
`unemployment_df` shows the percentage of unemployment in months of the
associated year. The result dataset `final_df` contains 4932
observations and 6 variables, with the year range from 1947 to 2015,
varibles contain year, month, president, closing value and unemployment
rate.
